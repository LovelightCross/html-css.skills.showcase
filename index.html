Dr. Andrews releases 'Throw Like A Pro' app to help reduce elbow injuries

Dr. James Andrews and Dr. Kevin Wilk have released the 'Throw Like A Pro' app. designed to help reduce the numberof elbow injuries, specifically Tommy John surgery. They "anticipate; a 60% decrease in overuse related to baseball injuries in the coming years" if the app is used properly. according to the release.
